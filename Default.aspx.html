KickAss Revival List (frontend)

This is a frontend for VandGD's awesome Old Roblox Revival List, that uses Javascript and Jquery to make the experience more enjoyable with features such as sorting, searching (by client years, name, and status), and Lormex playercounter integration.

Tips
To stay safe when interacting with revivals on this list, always make sure to do the following:
  1. Use a VPN, or even better, a VM.
  2. NEVER use your real name or anything tied to it (e.g. Emails).
  3. Don't lie about your age.
